Smart Money Scores

Each director-company pair is scored 0–10 on how often their on-market trades were followed by price movements in the correct direction (buys preceding rises, sells preceding falls) within 90 days. Scores are weighted by recency using exponential decay with an 18-month half-life — recent accurate trades count more than old ones.

Companies where 3 or more distinct directors all made open-market purchases in the last 90 days. High director count = strong insider conviction. Sorted by number of buying directors, then total buy value.

Smart Money Portfolio

Live paper trading portfolio that follows every Smart Money signal from today forward. Entry: $10,000 AUD at closing price when 2+ directors buy within 7 days. Exit: after 90 days, or immediately if 2+ directors sell. $100,000 starting capital, max 10 concurrent positions. Auto-updates every 2 minutes after each trade sync.
